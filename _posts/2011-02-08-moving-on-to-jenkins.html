So in case you haven't heard, there was some drama between the Hudson community and Oracle. I won't go into detail as there are plenty</a> of articles</a> about what went on.

All of the issues that were opened on the Hudson Jira</a> for the Perforce plugin are still open, but I'm unassigning those that are assigned to me. I'll continue maintaining the Perforce plugin against Jenkins, so bugs will be tracked there</a>, and any fixes I make will be made in the Jenkins repository. Note that the Jenkins version will</i> still work for Hudson until Oracle/Sonatype and/or the Jenkins Community decide to break cross-compatibility.

I'm not saying the Hudson version is dead, just that it doesn't have an active maintainer. Someone may take over maintaining it, but as far as I know, only one person has been assigned to working on Hudson and the hundreds of plugins written for it.
